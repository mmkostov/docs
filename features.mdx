# Artifimo Features

This section provides a detailed overview of Artifimo's features, categorized for easy reference.

## Core Features

### 1. AI-Powered Lead Generation

*   **Description:** Artifimo's core strength is its ability to automatically find and extract contact information from websites.  This is done using a combination of web scraping and artificial intelligence (AI), specifically Large Language Models (LLMs).
*   **How it works:**
    *   You provide one or more website URLs associated with a company.
    *   Artifimo's "crawler" visits these URLs and extracts the page content.
    *   The AI analyzes the page content, looking for patterns and contextual clues to identify names, emails, phone numbers, job titles, and social media links.
    *   The extracted data is validated and structured.
    *   The results are displayed and saved to your leads database.
*   **Key Benefits:**
    *   **Saves Time:** Automates the tedious manual process of finding contact information.
    *   **High Accuracy:**  Uses AI to ensure a high level of accuracy in identifying and verifying contact details.
    *   **Scalable:**  Can process multiple URLs simultaneously, allowing you to quickly gather leads from multiple pages on a website.
*   **Access:** Via the "Generate Leads" section of the app.

### 2. Leads Database

*   **Description:**  Artifimo provides a built-in database to store and manage all the leads you generate.
*   **Features:**
    *   **View Leads:**  Browse and search your leads, organized by company.
    *   **Edit Leads:** Manually update or correct lead information.
    *   **Filter Leads:**  Filter leads by company, name, or other criteria.
    *   **Export Leads:** Download your leads in CSV format for use in other applications (e.g., CRM, email marketing tools).
*   **Access:** Via the "View Leads" section of the app.

### 3. Company Management

*   **Description:**  Allows you to manage a list of companies you're targeting.
*   **Features:**
    *   **Add Company:** Create a new company record, including name and website.
    *   **Edit Company:** Update company details.
    *   **Select Company:**  Easily select a company when generating leads.
*   **Access:** Integrated into the "Generate Leads" page via a dropdown and an "Add Company" dialog.

## Account Management Features

### 4. User Account

*   **Description:**  Manage your personal account settings.
*   **Features:**
    *   **Update Profile:** Change your name and email address.
    *   **Change Password:** Update your account password.
    *   **Delete Account:** Permanently delete your account (this action is irreversible).
    *   **Sign Out:** Log out of your Artifimo account.
*   **Access:** Via the "Settings" -> "General" and "Settings" -> "Security" sections.

### 5. Team Management (for paid plans)

*   **Description:**  Invite and manage team members (requires being a team owner).
*   **Features:**
    *   **Invite Team Members:**  Send email invitations to colleagues to join your Artifimo team.
    *   **Assign Roles:** Set team member roles (e.g., "member," "owner").
    *   **Remove Team Members:** Remove users from your team.
*   **Access:** Via the "Settings" -> "Team" section.  Only team owners can invite new members.

### 6. Billing and Subscription Management (for paid plans)

*   **Description:**  Manage your subscription, payment methods, and billing information.
*   **Features:**
    *   **View Current Plan:**  See details of your current subscription plan.
    *   **Upgrade/Downgrade Plan:** Change your subscription plan.
    *   **Cancel Subscription:** Cancel your subscription.
    *   **Manage Payment Methods:** Update your payment information.
    *   **Access Billing Portal:**  Provides a link to the Stripe Customer Portal for more detailed billing management.
*   **Access:** Via the "Settings" -> "Billing" section.

### 7. Activity Log

*   **Description**: View a history of your recent actions within Artifimo.
*   **Features**:
    * See a chronological list of actions like signing in, updating account, generating leads, etc.
    * Displays the action, timestamp, and (if available) the IP address.
*   **Access**: Via the "Settings" -> "Activity" section.
