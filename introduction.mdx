# Welcome to Artifimo!

Artifimo is your AI-powered lead generation platform.  We help you find verified contact information from websites so you can focus on growing your business.  Instead of spending hours manually researching leads, Artifimo automates the process, delivering emails, phone numbers, social profiles, and job titles directly to you.

## What Artifimo Does

Artifimo leverages advanced AI and web scraping techniques to:

*   **Extract Contact Information:** Find emails, phone numbers, names, job titles, and social media links (LinkedIn, Twitter, etc.) from websites.
*   **Verify Data:**  Ensures a high level of accuracy by validating the contact information it finds.
*   **Organize Leads:** Provides a built-in dashboard to manage and track your newly discovered leads.
*   **Integrate with Your Workflow:**  Offers options to export leads to CSV or integrate with popular CRMs (coming soon).
*   **Save You Time:**  Automates the tedious process of manual lead research, freeing you up for outreach and closing deals.

## Who is Artifimo For?

Artifimo is ideal for:

*   **Sales Teams:**  Quickly build targeted contact lists for outreach campaigns.
*   **Marketing Teams:**  Enrich lead data and personalize marketing efforts.
*   **Recruiters:**  Find contact information for potential candidates.
*   **Founders & Entrepreneurs:**  Connect with investors, partners, and potential customers.
*   **Small Business Owners:**  Expand your reach without the need for expensive lead generation tools.
*   **Anyone** needing verified B2B contact information.

This documentation will guide you through setting up and using Artifimo effectively. Let's get started!
