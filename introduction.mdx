Welcome to Artifimo Chat, a powerful and intuitive chat application that leverages artificial intelligence to provide engaging and interactive conversations. Whether you're looking to have a casual chat, seek information, or explore creative ideas, Artifimo Chat is here to assist you.

## Key Features

Artifimo Chat offers a range of features designed to enhance your chatting experience:

1. **AI-Powered Conversations**: Engage in natural and intelligent conversations with our advanced AI models. Our models are trained on vast amounts of data, allowing them to provide relevant and contextual responses.

2. **Model Selection**: Choose from a variety of AI models to suit your preferences and needs. Each model has its own unique characteristics and capabilities, offering diverse conversation styles and expertise.

3. **Interactive Mode**: Experience a more dynamic and engaging chat with our interactive mode. In this mode, the AI actively participates in the conversation, asking questions, providing suggestions, and guiding the discussion.

4. **Web Browsing**: Enable the web browsing feature to allow the AI to access and incorporate information from the internet into your conversations. This feature enhances the AI's knowledge and enables it to provide more comprehensive and up-to-date responses.

5. **Message History**: Artifimo Chat keeps track of your conversation history, allowing you to easily review and refer back to previous messages. You can seamlessly continue conversations from where you left off.

6. **Customization Options**: Tailor your chat experience with various customization options. Adjust settings such as the AI's personality, response length, and more to suit your preferences.

## Getting Started

To start using Artifimo Chat, simply navigate to our website and create an account. Once logged in, you'll be greeted with a user-friendly interface that guides you through the chat process.

1. **Select a Model**: Choose the AI model you want to converse with from the available options. Each model has its own unique capabilities and personality.

2. **Start a New Chat**: Click on the "New Chat" button to initiate a new conversation. You can also continue an existing chat by selecting it from your chat history.

3. **Engage in Conversation**: Type your message in the input field and hit "Send" to start the conversation. The AI will respond based on the selected model and the context of the conversation.

4. **Explore Features**: Experiment with different features like interactive mode and web browsing to enhance your chat experience. Toggle these features on or off based on your preferences.

5. **Manage Chat History**: Access your chat history to review previous conversations, delete chats, or continue where you left off.
